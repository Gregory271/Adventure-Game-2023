Spooky Web Dilemma

Mr. Rivera is about to present todays topic in class but you are feeling the need to go to the restroom.

You decided to leave now. Upon entering the bathroom you notice that the stall has a screen on it that says "Please accept EULA before operating." Naturally you scroll past the boring stuff and hit accept to use the stall quickly as this is urgent, right?

You decided to stay in class! Mr. Rivera explained how End User License Agreements (EULA) are really important to read and understand since sometimes there are hidden rules that you dont notice while using a service. After the lesson you go to the bathroom and notice some new technology.

Since you didnt read the EULA, you didnt know that the stall was equipped with an NFC scanner that found your student ID from your card and sent you a 10 dollar bill. This specific stall is not free, who woulda known?!

Luckily you waited and understand that EULAs can be deceiving sometimes. So instead of accepting the EULA you found on the door of the bathroom stall, you decided to try somewhere else. W!
